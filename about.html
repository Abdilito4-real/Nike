Our Mission

At Nike, we believe in the power of sport to move the world. Our mission is to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete* in the world.

*If you have a body, you're an athlete.

Purpose

We champion continual progress for athletes and sport by taking action to help athletes reach their potential.

Sustainability

We're committed to protecting the future of sport through sustainable innovation and practices.

Community

We invest in communities where we live, work and play to expand access to sport and enable kids to reach their potential.

Our History

Founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, Nike officially became Nike, Inc. in 1971. The company takes its name from Nike, the Greek goddess of victory.

From our beginnings as a small distributor of Japanese running shoes, Nike has grown to become the world's leading innovator in athletic footwear, apparel, equipment and accessories.

Innovation

Nike has a long history of innovation, from the waffle sole to Air technology to Flyknit. We continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in sport through cutting-edge design and technology.

Innovation Timeline

1972

The Waffle Sole

Bill Bowerman creates the first Nike shoe with a waffle sole, revolutionizing running shoe traction.

1979

Air Cushioning

Nike Air technology debuts, providing superior cushioning for athletes.

1985

Air Jordan

The Air Jordan line launches, changing basketball shoes forever.
